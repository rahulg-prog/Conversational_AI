## Frequently Asked Questions
##### 1. How do I check if my vehicle has been recalled?
![Arrow](https://www.honda2wheelersindia.com/images/icons/accordion-down-arrow.svg)
To check the applicability of recall on your honda2wheelers, visit the recall page and select the category of vehicle. Next, you need to select the model and enter a 17-digit VIN/Chassis Number. Hit the "submit" button and the status of your vehicle will be displayed. Additionally, Honda will also proactively notify the public with press release, while dealers will notify with customers via customer letter/call/e-mail / SMS for the inspection and/or repair of their vehicle.
##### 2. What should I do if my vehicle is recalled?
![Arrow](https://www.honda2wheelersindia.com/images/icons/accordion-down-arrow.svg)
If your vehicle is identified under recall, you should notify the nearest HMSI dealership and schedule an appointment for the repairs.
##### 3. Is there a cost involved in the recall procedure?
![Arrow](https://www.honda2wheelersindia.com/images/icons/accordion-down-arrow.svg)
Irrespective of the warranty status of the honda2wheelers, the replacement will be carried out free-of-charge.
##### 4. Where will the replacement be carried out?
![Arrow](https://www.honda2wheelersindia.com/images/icons/accordion-down-arrow.svg)
All the honda2wheelers identified under recall will be attended at HMSI Authorized dealerships across the country.
##### 5. Can I take my vehicle anytime for replacement?
![Arrow](https://www.honda2wheelersindia.com/images/icons/accordion-down-arrow.svg)
To avoid any inconvenience or waiting, it is recommended to book your appointment in advance based on your availability.
##### 6. Will the suspected spare parts stock also be recalled?
![Arrow](https://www.honda2wheelersindia.com/images/icons/accordion-down-arrow.svg)
Honda is dedicated to providing safe and high-quality vehicles to customers. Therefore, all the suspected spare parts stock will also be replaced.
